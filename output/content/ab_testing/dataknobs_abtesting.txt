Web development,  A/B testing are 2 different fields.  They require different skill sets.
What if you can integrate this?
A business owner chose knobs - show layout 1 and 2 on landing page, do ab test and optimize for  user time spent on landing page.
With one knob change - you can generate web UI, experiment and validate/invalidate hypothesis.
<br/>
You want to show product recommendation at time of checkout and see whether it increase sales.
It require backend change, front end change and doing abtesting.
Using dataknobs you can switch on/off recommendation feature.  With other knob you can generate 2 variation and  do ab testing.
