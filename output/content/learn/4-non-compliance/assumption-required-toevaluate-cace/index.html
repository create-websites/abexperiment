"A/B Testing: Web vs Mobile Impact"

Assumption Impact on A/B Testing on Web Impact on A/B Testing on Mobile
Random Sampling Ensures that the test group and control group are representative of the overall population, leading to more accurate results. Same as web.
Statistical Significance Ensures that the results of the test are not due to chance, leading to more confidence in the results. Same as web.
Cookie Tracking Allows for tracking of user behavior across multiple sessions, leading to more accurate analysis of user behavior. May be impacted by limitations of mobile devices and apps, leading to less accurate tracking.
Device Compatibility Ensures that the test is compatible with all devices and browsers, leading to more accurate results. May be impacted by limitations of mobile devices and apps, leading to less accurate results.
Page Load Time Ensures that the test does not significantly impact page load time, leading to a better user experience. May be impacted by slower mobile internet speeds, leading to longer load times and a worse user experience.
